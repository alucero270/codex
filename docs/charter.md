# Project Charter

## Name

Strata

## Problem Statement

Organizations store knowledge across filesystems, repositories, and documents,
but lack a fast, unified way to retrieve relevant information.

## Users

- Engineers
- Technical teams
- Internal knowledge workers

## Why Now

Knowledge is fragmented across tools. Teams need a centralized, fast, and
reliable retrieval layer before layering AI systems on top.

## Definition of Success

- Users can retrieve relevant artifacts in < 2 queries
- Search latency under 1 second for most queries
- System works without requiring AI components
- System can later integrate AI for enhanced retrieval
